Privacy Policy for PW GENERATE AND COPY

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

1. Overview

PW GENERATE AND COPY is a Chrome extension that generates secure passwords locally in your browser. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Generated passwords
  • User preferences or settings
  • Browsing history
  • Personal data of any kind
  • Usage statistics or analytics

3. How the Extension Works

PW GENERATE AND COPY operates entirely locally within your browser:

  • All password generation occurs on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No network connections are made
  • Passwords are only copied to your clipboard temporarily

4. Permissions

The extension requires no special permissions and does not access:

  • Your browsing data
  • Website content
  • Personal files
  • Other applications

5. Data Storage

The extension does not store any data persistently. All generated passwords are temporary and exist only in your clipboard until you copy something else.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer information.

9. Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store policies
  • Manifest V3 security standards
  • Privacy best practices
